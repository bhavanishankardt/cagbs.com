About
A focused, independent practice.

Bhavani Shankar Giddaluru is a Qualified Chartered Accountant in practice, with a dedicated focus on the taxation of businesses and individuals across both direct and indirect tax.

The practice spans hands-on advisory and litigation — GST refunds and show-cause notices, income-tax assessments and appeals — alongside the recurring work that keeps clients compliant: return filings across income-tax and GST, tax audits and statutory audit support, and day-to-day reconciliations. Each engagement is handled personally, with the depth a specialist brings and the responsiveness of a small firm.

Based in Proddatur, Andhra Pradesh, with services provided remotely to clients across India.
